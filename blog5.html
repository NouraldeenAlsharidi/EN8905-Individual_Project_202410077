Activity 1: Wind Turbine Power Output Calculation

Method

Using published wind speed data for Bahrain's offshore zone (Gulf of Bahrain, northern sector) from the Saudi/Bahrain joint Wind Atlas (IRENA 2023), I calculated the expected annual energy output of a hypothetical 10-turbine offshore wind demonstration farm using the standard wind power equation.

Wind Power Formula:
P = ½ · ρ · A · v³ · Cₚ

Where: ρ = air density (1.225 kg/m³), A = rotor swept area (m²), v = wind speed (m/s), Cₚ = power coefficient (Betz limit max = 0.593; practical ≈ 0.45)

Calculation Steps

Step 1 — Power at mean wind speed:
P = ½ × 1.16 × 21,887 × (8.2)³ × 0.45
P = ½ × 1.16 × 21,887 × 551.4 × 0.45
P = 3,146 kW = 3.15 MW per turbine

Step 2 — Capacity factor correction:
CF = 0.40 (40% — realistic for Gulf offshore conditions)

Step 3 — Annual Energy Output (AEO) per turbine:
AEO = Rated Power × CF × 8,760 h/yr
AEO = 8,000 kW × 0.40 × 8,760 = 28,032 MWh/yr per turbine

Step 4 — Farm total (10 turbines):
AEO_farm = 10 × 28,032 = 280,320 MWh/yr = 280.3 GWh/yr

Step 5 — CO₂ avoided vs. gas (490 g/kWh):
CO₂_avoided = 280,320 MWh × 490 kg/MWh = 137,357 tonnes CO₂/yr

Activity 2: Industrial Energy Audit at SABIC (ICS Department)

Method

As part of my work in the Instrument & Control Systems (ICS) department at SABIC, I conducted a 5-day energy consumption audit of our department's instrumentation and control room equipment. I used a calibrated power meter (Fluke 435-II power quality analyser) to log real-time consumption of 6 load categories.

Results & Link to Energy Assessment

Annual consumption: 633.6 × 365 = 231,264 kWh/yr = 231.3 MWh/yr

CO₂ from current gas grid: 231,264 × 0.490 = 113 tCO₂/yr from this one department alone

If powered by offshore wind (12 g/kWh): 231,264 × 0.012 = 2.8 tCO₂/yr — a 97.5% reduction

This practical finding directly supports the energy system comparison: transitioning SABIC's ICS department (and by extension, the wider SABIC facility) to wind-powered electricity would eliminate over 110 tonnes of CO₂ per department per year — a tangible, measurable contribution to SDG 13.

Evidence: Fluke 435-II data logs (5-day period, 1-minute intervals), load category breakdown spreadsheet, site photos taken with department manager consent. Raw data available on request. Measurements taken: 20–24 June 2026.
